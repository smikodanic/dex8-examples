027ff_next

FunctionFlow next() method.
Stop execution of all funcs functions in serial(funcs) method and continue with next serial or parallel method.

In this example there are 3 serial methods which executes f1, f2 and f3 serially.
Function f2 has next() so f3 is never executed.
